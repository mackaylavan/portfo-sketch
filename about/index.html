About Me

I design for the digital world. By combining a visual design background with a love for tinkering with code and sensors, I create playful experiences and interfaces that are accessible to all without feeling overly generic.

After finishing high school, I moved to the Netherlands to push myself out of my comfort zone and study design with other students from all over the world. Mixing ideas with other designers and clients from all walks of life became my practice, and I constantly seek out new people to work with and learn from. Our stories make us unique, and I aim to incorporate the one-of-a-kind story of each client I work with into the work we create together. Want to know more about me?
